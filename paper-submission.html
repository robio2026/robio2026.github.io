PAPER SUBMISSION

Paper Submission

The lEEE ROBIO 2025 is now calling for initial submissions of full papers or extended abstracts before the deadline.

Submission system link: https://softconf.com/p/robio2026/

Submission Guidelines

Full paper: A full paper (6-8 pages) submission will be considered for presentation, best paper awards, and involved in proceedings for publication. For accepted papers with more than 6 pages, the author will be required to pay extra pages in final submission.

Submission requirements:

  • Authors should prepare their papers or abstracts based on the LaTex template or Microsoft Word template:
    Format Guidelines: https://ras.papercept.net/conferences/support/support.php
    LaTex: https://ras.papercept.net/conferences/support/tex.php
    Word: https://ras.papercept.net/conferences/support/word.php
  • Prepare an abstract and keywords that accurately describe the contribution.
  • Ensure figures, tables, equations, and references are legible in the final PDF.
  • Check that all required authors and contact details are entered in the submission form.
  • Retain the confirmation email and paper ID generated by the system after submission.
  • Monitor the website and conference notices for updates on deadlines and final submission instructions.
